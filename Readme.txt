Welcome to my portfolio website! This website serves as a showcase for my work, skills, and experience, providing an overview of my professional background, projects, and contact information. It is designed to give visitors insight into my capabilities and accomplishments, with a focus on usability and a visually engaging layout.


The purpose of this portfolio website is to:

Display my personal and professional projects, including descriptions, screenshots, and links.
Highlight my skills and expertise in relevant fields, including technical and creative abilities.
Provide an easy way for potential employers, clients, or collaborators to contact me.
Serve as a personal hub for my work, allowing visitors to explore my projects and get to know me as a professional.

This portfolio website is built with the following technologies:

HTML5: Provides the fundamental structure and content layout.
CSS3: Enhances the visual styling with custom designs, animations, and responsive layouts.

The portfolio website includes the following features:

Responsive Design: Fully responsive layout, providing an optimized experience on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Interactive Project Showcase: Includes a dedicated section with cards or modals for each project, allowing users to view project details, screenshots.
Skill Section: A visual representation of my skills and proficiencies, often displayed with progress bars, and icons.
Contact Form: A functional contact link that directly to my whatsapp.
Smooth Scrolling & Animations: Smooth scrolling effects and animations enhance user engagement and navigation.
